Good Bye Pastor Brian

This week the minister who baptized you last April announced to the congregation that he will be leaving the Mount Pleasant Community Church to lead a different church in Arizona. I thought that this news was very poignant, as your baptism was a very special day. Family from your mom’s side and our neighbors the Hollidays were there to witness your baptism. Afterwards, we ate lunch together at Pizza Hut.

Your brother, sister, and I had been attending the MPCC for a few years, so it seemed appropriate for you to be baptized there. It was also a joy to have our family with us to celebrate the day.
